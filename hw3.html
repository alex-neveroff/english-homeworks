What is the most important to you in a job?

Work/Life balance

Two weeks ago I would have said that the most important is Job security because it means employees don’t lose their job with a very high probability.

But now I think differently.

Two last weeks I worked a couple of hours at night and a couple of hours during the day and due to an emergency power outage, I didn't know when these hours would come. I couldn't relax even at night, couldn't properly prepare for work and study so I'm disappointed with my results for this period.

So I think that the employees must have sufficient time for their own lives. I mean that harmony is needed. Time for work, time for study, time for family and leisure, including sleep. Yin and yang. Then their efficiency at work will be the highest. Perfect work/life balance almost guarantees high performance at work.
