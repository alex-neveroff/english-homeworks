Why did you leave your previous job?

My last job was related to copywriting and media content, which I must upload on sites through the admin panel.

I like my last job and wonderful team, but I lost my motivation to do that job. I understood that I want not only work with websites but create and develop them. And in the process of JavaScript studying, I realized that solving such tasks is mine.

So, I want not just to change jobs, but to change the profession.

What are you looking for in your next job and employer?

Firstly, I want to program as much as possible. It would be ideal to get a lot of interesting and challenging tasks, but I also like a routine one too.

Secondly, I hope for the support of the employer in cases where I, as a beginner, can not cope with the work as quickly as an experienced professional.

Thirdly, I would want to work with a friendly team. I treat people with respect and positivity and would like reciprocity. Actually, that's usually what happens.
