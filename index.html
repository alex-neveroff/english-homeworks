What do you do to improve your English?

About three weeks ago I started to learn English using ED Courses mobile application. It makes possible to revise the grammar and expand my vocabulary.

Since our lesson last Saturday and aadvice from Julia I have started to use another application called Duolingo too. It is simpler and more playful, but gives a small chance to practice speaking. I spend about an hour on the both applications every day.

Also I read simple books on English. I try do it every evening before going to bed. Now I'm reading “Lost world” by Arthur Conan Doyle.

In addition, I want to improve my listening skills and took audio lessons from Vitaliy Levental. It’s a set of dialogues on everyday topics, not very hard, but they speak in a natural way and with a natural pace. I like this course, but to my great regret I can’t donate much of time to it and study about twice a week only.

However, I try to learn English every day anyway, I think, I need to reconsider the lessons plan to improve my results.
