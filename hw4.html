What are your career development goals?

My main goal in my career is to get a job as a junior developer in a big IT company. To achieve this, I’m going to expand my knowledge and improve my hard and soft skills.

So, I’m taking a course in fullstack development now and I code as often as I can. I do not only do individual projects, but team ones, too. It is so involving! I like when all team members distribute the tasks among themselves, deploy their best skills, brainstorm and architect the final decision.

So far, I have done two team projects and three personal ones.

Also, I find it necessary to constantly improve my English, so I study with a tutor, read literature in English and expand my vocabulary.
